About this RAIN unofficial site

On April 5, 2003, Fumiyuki, the vocalist of RAIN, passed away. For this reason, unfortunately, the complete revival of RAIN is no longer possible. So I created this site in the hope that there would be a place where people could remember RAIN, at least a little.

If you have any comments, suggestions, or additional information about this site, please contact me at eternalseason@hotmail.com. I will update the information as soon as possible. If you have any other questions or comments, please feel free to contact me.
