2000.8.28 Reikai Jubaku (麗界呪縛) at Maebashi (前橋) club FLEEZ

Other bands

Celestia, Despairs Ray, Crecien de Rona, S'aLateal, De-ARK

Set list

1. Shimobe (隷)
2. su.i.cide
MC
3. Kinu no Ito to Kumo no Ito (絹の絲と蜘の意図)
4. less
5. Gyou (行)
6. Fusei Reiju (浮生麗終)

This day is my first visit to live house and RAIN's live!

RAIN performed at the end. When RAIN came on stage, there was a rope stretched across the stage. The costumes on this day were almost the same as the ones on the jacket of "Kinu-Kumo" demo tape.

The song "less" had a sad feeling to it. The stage was lit up with blue lights during the intro part of "less", and I thought it matched the mood of this song.

By the end of the live, I was completely captivated by RAIN♥
